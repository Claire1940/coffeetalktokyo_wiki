<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering the Art of Brewing">

The heart of the gameplay remains the brewing mini-game. You are tasked with combining three ingredients to create the perfect drink. In *Coffee Talk Tokyo*, the ingredient list has been expanded to include more regional flavors, allowing for a wider variety of secret recipes.

**How to Use the New Stencil System:**

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Base">
    Choose your primary ingredient (Coffee, Tea, Green Tea, or Chocolate) and two secondary ingredients as per the customer's request.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply the Stencil">
    Before adding the final touches, select the "Say it with Stencils" option. Choose from various patterns like hearts, stars, or traditional Japanese motifs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sprinkle and Etch">
    Sprinkle the cocoa or matcha powder over the stencil. You can then use the traditional milk pouring and etching tools to further customize the design.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Serve and Observe">
    Deliver the drink to the customer. Their reaction will depend on how well you matched their requested flavor profile (Sweet, Bitter, Cool, or Warm).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Tip">
Experimenting in the "Free Brew" mode is the best way to discover hidden recipes without the pressure of an active customer order.
</Callout>
