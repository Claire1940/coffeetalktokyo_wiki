Coffee Talk Tokyo on Nintendo Switch builds upon the established mechanics of its predecessors while introducing new elements to keep the experience fresh. The central loop revolves around brewing drinks, listening to customer stories, and making choices that shape their destinies.

### Brewing Beverages
The drink-making system is intuitive yet impactful. You'll combine three ingredients to create specific hot or cold drinks. Correctly guessing and serving the desired beverage can increase a customer's "friendship level" and unlock new story paths.
