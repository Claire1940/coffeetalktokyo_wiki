<Section icon={Sparkles} title="New Brewing Mechanics and Drink Recipes">

While the core loop of brewing drinks remains familiar, Coffee Talk Tokyo introduces several mechanical upgrades to reflect its new setting. The most significant addition is the inclusion of cold drinks. Since the game is set during a brutal Tokyo summer, customers will frequently request refreshing beverages to escape the heat.

The latte art system has also seen an upgrade with the introduction of "Sprinkle Stencils." This allows baristas to create intricate designs more easily before adding manual touches with milk and etching tools. Mastering these tools is essential for influencing character moods and unlocking specific branching paths in the story.

| Drink Category | Base Ingredients | Effect on Customers |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hot Coffee** | Coffee, Milk, Sugar | Comforting, encourages long stories |
| **Cold Brews** | Coffee, Ice, Mint | Refreshing, helps characters focus |
| **Tea Blends** | Green Tea, Ginger, Honey | Healing, used for emotional distress |
| **Specialty** | Matcha, Chocolate, Stencils | Creative, unlocks hidden dialogue |

<Section icon={Zap} title="Mastering the Tomodachill Social Network">

Tomodachill returns as the primary way to track character progression outside of the café. In Coffee Talk Tokyo, this system has been expanded with "Clickable Hashtags." This feature acts as a narrative trail of breadcrumbs, allowing you to discover "hidden" posts from characters you haven't officially met or followed yet.

Following these hashtags is crucial for players aiming for the "Best" endings. Some posts contain vital clues about a character's true desires or upcoming events that they might not mention directly across the counter.
