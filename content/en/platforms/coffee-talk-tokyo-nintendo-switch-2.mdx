### Latte Art with Stencils

Elevate your coffee creations with the new sprinkle stencils. This feature allows for greater personalization and creativity in your latte art.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select a Drink">
    Choose the base drink for your customer's order.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply Stencil">
    Select a sprinkle stencil design to apply to the drink's surface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Add Milk & Etch">
    Carefully add milk and use etching techniques to refine your latte art, making each drink unique.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success">
