Andrew "AJ" Jeremy, the acclaimed composer behind the soundtracks for *Coffee Talk* and *Coffee Talk Episode 2*, returns to helm the musical direction for *Coffee Talk Tokyo*. This dual strategy ensures both novelty and a comforting sense of connection for long-time fans. The game's setting in Tokyo, a city where old and new coexist, provided the perfect canvas for this musical evolution.
