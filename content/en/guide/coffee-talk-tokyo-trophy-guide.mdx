<Section icon={Music} title="Unlocking the Music Lover Achievement">
The "Music Lover" achievement requires you to listen to all the songs in the game. While you could let the soundtrack cycle naturally, there's a much faster way using your in-game phone.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Shuffld">
    Open your in-game phone and navigate to the "Shuffld" application. This is your music player.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="View All Songs">
    Within Shuffld, select the option to view "All Songs." This will display a complete list of the game's soundtrack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cycle Through Tracks">
    Select each song in the list. You only need to let each track play for a few seconds, enough to hear its opening notes, before skipping to the next one.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Achievement Unlocked">
    Once you have cycled through all unique songs, the "Music Lover" achievement will pop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
This method ensures you don't miss any hidden tracks or wait for the game to play them organically, making it a quick and easy unlock.
</Callout>

</Section>
