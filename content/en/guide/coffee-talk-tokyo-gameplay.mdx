<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand the Order">
    Listen carefully to customer requests. They might directly ask for a specific drink, or subtly hint at their preferences through their dialogue. Consider their current mood or story arc.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Ingredients">
    Choose a base (e.g., coffee, tea, green tea, milk), a main ingredient, and two additional ingredients from your pantry. New cold options are available, like ice.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Craft the Drink">
    Combine the selected ingredients using the brewing interface. Some drinks require specific combinations to be successful.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Latte Art (Optional)">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Serve and Observe">
    Serve the drink to the customer and observe their reaction. A well-made drink will often open up new dialogue options and deepen your connection with them.
  </Step>
</Steps>
