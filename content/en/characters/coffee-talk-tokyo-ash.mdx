The beloved visual novel series returns with **Coffee Talk Tokyo**, inviting players to step behind the counter of a cozy late-night café in the bustling heart of Tokyo. This newest entry, released on May 21, 2026, promises familiar comforting gameplay alongside entirely new characters and branching storylines. As a barista, your role is to brew the perfect hot or cold beverages for a diverse clientele of humans and yōkai, all while listening to their heartfelt stories and subtly influencing their lives through meaningful conversations.

The game maintains the series' signature lo-fi beats and atmospheric charm, providing a relaxing yet engaging experience. Your choices, both in drink preparation and dialogue, will directly impact the outcomes for your customers, leading to multiple unique endings.

The narrative of **Coffee Talk Tokyo** is driven by your interactions, making every playthrough unique. The game emphasizes deeply personal, branching stories where your dialogue choices and drink preparations directly influence character outcomes and unlock different endings.

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Brew Thoughtfully">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose Your Words Wisely">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master Tomodachill">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Witness the Outcome">
    Your cumulative decisions throughout the game will culminate in one of several possible endings for each character, reflecting the impact you've had on their lives.
  </Step>
