<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: How to Brew the Perfect Special Drink">

Unlocking the 54 special recipes in your Brewpad requires precision. If you deviate by even one ingredient, you will end up with a "generic" drink (like a "Ginger Soymilk") instead of a named special (like "STMJ"). Follow this workflow to ensure every cup is a masterpiece.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Temperature">
    Start by choosing **Hot** or **Cold**. This is the most common point of failure for recipes like the "Sunset Fuji" (Cold) or "Gala Had" (Hot).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Your Base">
    Select one of the six bases from the top row. For creamy drinks, remember that Soymilk is your go-to.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Add the Primary Flavor">
    Add the second ingredient. This can be another measure of your base (to intensify it) or a fruit/spice. This ingredient carries the most weight in the flavor profile.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finish with the Secondary Ingredient">
    Add the final touch. This is where you can add **Ice Cream** or **Whipped Cream**, which are only available in this third slot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Review and Serve">
    Check the flavor bars one last time. If you realize you've made a mistake, use the **Reset** button before hitting "Brew."
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Limited Trashes">
You can only throw away five drinks per in-game day. Use them wisely! If you are hunting for a specific recipe in Endless Mode, saving your game before a complex brew is a highly recommended tactic.
</Callout>

</Section>
