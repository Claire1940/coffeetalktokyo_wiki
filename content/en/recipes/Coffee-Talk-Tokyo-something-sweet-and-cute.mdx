<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step: Making a Sweet & Cute Drink with Latte Art">
Follow these steps to create a delightful drink that satisfies the "sweet and cute" criteria, especially for customers like Ayame.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Base">
    For a sweet and milky drink, a **Chocolate** base is often a good starting point. Alternatively, a **Soymilk** base can also work well, especially if the customer has dietary preferences or you want to lean into the milky aspect more heavily.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Add Sweeteners">
    The "sweet" component is crucial. Common sweeteners include **Honey** or **Lychee**. For Ayame's request, combining Chocolate with Honey is an excellent choice for a rich, sweet flavor profile.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Incorporate Milkiness">
    If "heaps of milk" is requested, make **Soymilk** a primary or secondary ingredient. In Coffee Talk Tokyo, many recipes now account for soy milk, making it a versatile option. Ensure the drink has a high milk content.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Latte Art (Stencil)">
    This is where the "cute" and "pretty stencil" aspects come in. After brewing the drink, navigate to the latte art interface. Select a stencil design and apply it. Then, carefully pour milk to fill the design or etch additional details if desired. This is essential for achievements like "The Whole Picture" and "It's a Masterpiece!".
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Serve and Observe">
    Present the finished drink to your customer. Pay close attention to their dialogue and reactions. A positive response indicates you've met their expectations, contributing to your progress towards the "Voted Barista Of The Year" achievement.
  </Step>
</Steps>
