<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Hojicha and its Variants">
Hojicha is a Japanese green tea that is roasted over charcoal, giving it a distinctive nutty and earthy flavor. Unlike other green teas, its roasting process reduces caffeine content, making it a popular choice for evening consumption. In Coffee Talk Tokyo, Hojicha is a versatile base, forming several unique drinks.
