<Section icon={Target} title="The Coffee Talk Tokyo Warm Hojicha Recipe">

To successfully brew the standard **coffee talk tokyo warm hojicha recipe**, you must focus on the purity of the roasted tea. Unlike complex lattes or fruit-infused teas, the basic Hojicha drink emphasizes the quality of the roast itself. This drink is a staple for those who prefer traditional Japanese flavors without the sweetness of honey or the heaviness of milk.

In the 2026 edition of the game, the brewing interface remains intuitive but requires precision. Selecting the "Hot" temperature setting is the most critical first step. If you accidentally select the cold option, you will end up with an Iced Hojicha, which may not satisfy a customer specifically asking for something "warm" or "toasty."

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select the Temperature">
    Begin by selecting the **Hot** base on your brewing machine. The game differentiates strictly between hot and cold outcomes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Add the First Ingredient">
    Select **Hojicha** as your primary base. This sets the earthy foundation of the drink.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Add the Second Ingredient">
    Add a second portion of **Hojicha**. This increases the "Warmth" and "Bitterness" bars in the drink's profile.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Add the Final Ingredient">
    Complete the recipe with a third portion of **Hojicha**. This solidifies the drink as the "Hojicha" special recipe in your Brewpad.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Brewing Error">
Do not add any milk or sweeteners if you are aiming for the pure Hojicha recipe. Adding even a single drop of Soymilk will turn the drink into a different variation or a standard "Hojicha Latte" which won't trigger the special drink unlock.
</Callout>

</Section>
