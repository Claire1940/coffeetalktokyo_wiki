The brewing process follows a strict three-slot system: a **Base**, a **Primary Ingredient**, and a **Secondary Ingredient**. In *Coffee Talk Tokyo*, you must first toggle the temperature switch before adding your components. If you follow a recipe perfectly but select the wrong temperature, you will end up with a "Standard" drink rather than a "Special" one, which often prevents characters from opening up during deep conversations.

<Callout type="tip" title="Barista Secret">
If a customer asks for something "refreshing" or "chilled," always default to the **Cold** setting. Conversely, if they mention "warmth" or "comfort," stay on the **Hot** setting. Some special recipes only exist in one temperature state.
</Callout>
